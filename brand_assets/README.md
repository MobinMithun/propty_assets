# Brand Assets & Identity

This directory contains the foundational visual and strategic assets for **Propty By JCX**. 

## 🏆 Core Identity (from `brand_assets.jpeg`)
- **Primary Slogan**: "The First AI-Backed Real Estate Company."
- **Mission**: "Smarter Search. Better Decisions. Bigger Future."
- **Brand Promise**: "AI understands data. We understand you."
- **Key Metrics**:
  - **95%** More Accurate Recommendations with AI Insights.
  - **70%** Time Saved in Property Search and Shortlisting.

## 🎨 Visual Guidelines
- **Primary Color**: Deep Vibrant Purple/Indigo (The "Propty Purple").
- **Accent Colors**: Neon Violet, Emerald Green (for success/verification), and Slate Navy.
- **Style**: Premium, High-Tech, Transparent, and Human-Centered.
- **Imagery**: Urban landscapes (Dhaka/Gulshan), tech-integrated lifestyles, and professional collaboration.

## 🚀 Key Features to Highlight
- **AI-Powered Search**: Intent-based and predictive.
- **AI Pricing & Market Insights**: Real-time valuation and trend analysis.
- **AI Assistant**: 24/7 intelligent support and document flow.
- **Transparency**: Detailed analysis and verified listing badges.

## 📂 File Directory
- `brand_assets.jpeg`: The primary brand poster and identity reference.
- `Propty Final powerpoint.pdf`: Full strategic deck.
- `slides/`: Individual slide exports from the powerpoint.
- `intelligence/`: Market research and roadmap updates (Living Documentation).
